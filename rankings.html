Scoring Methodology & Impact Rubric

Total Circle Participants

30%

how many circles have individuals from that institution/university actively participated in since the intialization of the STAR Network

Total Circles Run

10%

how many circles have been run in the past, are actively running and will run in future led by leaders from a particular university

STAR Awards Won

30%

how many STAR affiliated awards (especially the Global Education for Peace award) have been won by that university in the past

STAR Circle Engagement

30%

what was the engagement & responsiveness rate evaluated by circle leaders for the participants from a certain university
